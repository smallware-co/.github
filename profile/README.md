# Smallware Co.

Small tools. Real utility. Bring your own AI key.

All tools are free to use, open source, and run entirely in your browser. No backend. No data collection. Your API key never leaves your device.

---

## // live tools

### [Content Brief Builder](https://github.com/smallware-co/content-brief-builder)
Keyword in. Full content brief out — audience, intent, H2 structure, title options, related terms, and word count. Works with Gemini, OpenAI, or Anthropic.

### [Reddit Topic Finder](https://github.com/smallware-co/reddit-topic-finder)
Find blog topics people are actually asking about. Enter a niche, get 12 validated topic ideas pulled from real Reddit question patterns. Powered by Z.AI GLM.

### [Trend Finder](https://github.com/smallware-co/trend-finder)
Multi-country trending topic finder. Pick up to 3 countries, get 20 long-tail, low-competition blog topics per location — grouped by theme, exportable to CSV. Powered by Z.AI GLM.

### [Power Planner](https://github.com/smallware-co/powerplanner)
AI-assisted planning that breaks goals into tasks that actually get done.
